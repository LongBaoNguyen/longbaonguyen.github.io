AP Computer Science Principles

This is a resource for teachers and students for AP Computer Science Principles. The official language for the course is Python. In addition to meeting the AP Computer Science Principles learning objectives, the course will also cover 1) writing arcade games including platformers 2) large text processing(e.g. analyzing Shakespeare's works) 3) audio processing with the Discrete Fourier Transform 4) image recognition with neural networks 5) basic data science.
